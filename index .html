About This Program

Welcome to the Financial Literacy App! This tool is designed to help you manage your finances more effectively by providing the following features:

  • Budget Calculator: Allows you to calculate and manage your monthly budget by comparing income and expenses.
  • Savings Goal Calculator: Helps you set and track savings goals by calculating how much you need to save each month to reach your target.
  • Financial Tips: Offers practical advice on budgeting, saving, investing, and avoiding debt to improve your financial health.

How to Use:

  • Navigate to the Budget Calculator to enter your income and expenses. Click "Calculate Budget" to see your remaining balance.
  • Use the Savings Goal Calculator to input your savings goal and the number of months you plan to save. Click "Calculate Savings" to see the required monthly savings.
  • Check out the Financial Tips section for advice on managing your finances.

Financial Tips

  • Create and stick to a budget.
  • Set financial goals and track your progress.
  • Save regularly, even if it's a small amount.
  • Avoid unnecessary debt and manage existing debt wisely.
  • Invest in your education and skills for better earning potential.
